# About Me


## Introduction

Hello! I'm Damien Babin, a DevOps engineer focused on DevSecOps, cloud infrastructure, automation, and secure software delivery. My background combines Azure administration, Terraform, CI/CD, containerization, Security+, an active DoD Secret Clearance, and four years of Marine Corps leadership experience.

## Background

I served in the United States Marine Corps as an Anti-Tank Missileman from June 2020 to June 2024 at Camp Lejeune, NC. During that time, I led, trained, and mentored teams while holding billets including Fire Team Leader, Platoon Sergeant, Training NCO, Combat Marksmanship Coach, Radio Operator, HMMWV/JLTV Driver, and Combat Lifesaver. That experience shaped how I approach technical work: accountable, security-minded, calm under pressure, and focused on operational readiness.
